ENIZMA Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

ENIZMA is a text and media encryption app developed by ENIZMA Team. This Privacy Policy explains how ENIZMA handles information when you use the app.

Information ENIZMA Processes

ENIZMA can process text, images, audio, video, documents, encrypted files, app settings, usage limits, and purchase status. Encryption and decryption are designed to run on your device.

ENIZMA does not require you to create an account.

Secret Keys

Your secret keys are used only to encrypt or decrypt content on your device. ENIZMA does not intentionally collect, upload, sell, or share your secret keys.

ENIZMA cannot recover your encrypted content if you lose or forget the correct secret key.

Text, Media, and Files

Text and media selected for encryption or decryption are processed locally on your device. Encrypted and decrypted files may be saved in ENIZMA app storage or shared by you through Android sharing features.

If you choose to open, share, import, or export files, Android and the receiving apps may process those files according to their own privacy policies.

Advertising

ENIZMA may use Google AdMob to show ads, including rewarded ads. Google may collect device identifiers, ad interaction data, approximate location, diagnostics, and other information as described in Google's policies.

Google privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Purchases

ENIZMA may use Google Play Billing for Premium and Pro purchases. Purchase processing is handled by Google Play. ENIZMA may receive purchase status, product identifiers, purchase tokens, and related transaction data needed to unlock paid features.

Purchase Verification and App Integrity

To protect paid features from fraud, ENIZMA may verify purchases with a server using Google Play Developer API and may use Play Integrity API. This verification can process purchase tokens, product IDs, package name, app integrity signals, device integrity signals, and nonces.

This verification is used to confirm that purchases are valid and that the app environment is trusted.

Local Storage

ENIZMA may store settings, language preference, theme preference, encrypted/decrypted file records, usage counts, temporary premium status, purchase status, and app history on your device.

You can delete app data through Android system settings or uninstall the app.

Security

ENIZMA includes local protections against tampering, debugging, rooted environments, emulator use, and unauthorized feature access. No mobile app can guarantee complete protection against all attacks.

Children's Privacy

ENIZMA is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use apps with ads, purchases, or encryption tools without supervision.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version should be made available in the app listing, website, or app settings.

Contact
